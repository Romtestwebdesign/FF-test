Classes at Forward Fitness

Online and In-Person Classes

Individual Training Group Training
Individual Training is a one-on-one class between you and one of our certified personal trainers. Our personal trainers will help you reach goals that you set, and push you to your limits. This program lets you choose your time slots through our trainer's schedule, and provides both online and in person flexibility. Group Training is a program which allows you to train together with a group of peers. Twice a week, you and the others who signed up for the same group program meet at Forward Fitness, or online at your home. You perform excercises as a group, and learn excercise routines and practices.
