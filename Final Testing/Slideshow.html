TUYU

Japanese Rock Band

TUYU has been one of my favorite bands for a long
time standing. The band plays Japanese rock songs,
one of them being "Being low as dirt, taking what's
important from me". This was one of the first songs
I heard from them, and it had instantly caught my
attention.

Eve

Japanese Pop Soloist

Eve is a Japanese pop singer and songwriter. The
first song I ever heard from himn was "Heart Forcast".
I had been on youtube browsing videos, when this
came up in my feed. The thumbnail instantly caught
my attention, and as I watched the music video i was
stunned. Never had i seen a such beautiful animation
done for a single solo song. I've been listening to his
music since then.

PinnochioP

Japanese Vocaloid
Producer

PinnochioP is a Japanese Vocaloid Producer and
songwriter who creates Japanese Pop songs. I
had been listening to his music here and there,
but I never really followed him too closely as an
artist. However, when he released the single
"God-ish", I was hooked. To me the song was so
unique, and really separated him from other
music producers.
